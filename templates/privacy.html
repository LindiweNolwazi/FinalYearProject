Privacy Policy

Your privacy matters to us. This policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your data.

1. Information We Collect

We collect personal information provided during registration and use of the platform, including:

  • Students: Name, student number, email, academic records, proof of registration, and tuition details.
  • Sponsors: Name/organization details, email, contact numbers, and sponsorship preferences.
  • Admins: Limited information for system management and verification.

2. How We Use Your Information

Your data is used for:

  • Student verification by university administrators.
  • Connecting sponsors with eligible students.
  • Processing secure tuition payments.
  • Sending notifications about application status or new funding opportunities.

3. Data Protection

We implement strict technical and organizational measures to safeguard your data, including encryption, secure payment gateways, and role-based access controls.

4. Sharing of Information

We do not sell or trade your personal information. Data may only be shared with:

  • University administrators for verification.
  • Payment processors for secure transactions.
  • Regulatory authorities if required by law.

5. Cookies and Analytics

We may use cookies and analytics tools to improve platform performance and user experience. You can disable cookies in your browser settings.

6. User Rights

  • Access your personal data.
  • Request correction of inaccurate information.
  • Request deletion of your data (subject to legal and financial obligations).

7. Data Retention

We retain user data only as long as necessary for funding processes, compliance, or as required by law.

8. Third-Party Links

Our platform may contain links to third-party sites (e.g., sponsor websites). We are not responsible for their privacy practices.

9. Updates to Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated periodically. Users will be notified of changes via email or platform alerts.

10. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at: support@gofundmeconnect.edu.za
