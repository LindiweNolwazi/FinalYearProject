Actors and Their Roles in the System

Students

Primary beneficiaries of the platform

Students are at the heart of the GoFundMe Connect platform. They create profiles, apply for funding, and receive financial support for their education.

Key Responsibilities

  • Create and maintain accurate profiles
  • Upload required documentation
  • Apply for SRC funding and sponsorships
  • Maintain academic performance standards
  • Communicate with sponsors when needed

Platform Capabilities

  • SRC Fund application management
  • Sponsorship search and applications
  • Document upload and management
  • Application status tracking
  • Communication tools

Sponsors

Providers of financial support

Sponsors include individuals, corporations, alumni, and NGOs who want to support students' education through financial contributions.

Key Responsibilities

  • Register and verify sponsor profile
  • Browse and select students to support
  • Provide funding through secure channels
  • Monitor impact of their contributions
  • Maintain ethical sponsorship practices

Platform Capabilities

  • Student search and filtering tools
  • Secure payment processing
  • Sponsorship management dashboard
  • Impact tracking and reporting

Administrators

Platform managers and verifiers

Administrators ensure the platform operates smoothly, verify information, prevent fraud, and facilitate the connection between students and sponsors.

Key Responsibilities

  • Verify student eligibility and documents
  • Monitor and prevent fraudulent activity
  • Manage funding allocation
  • Generate system reports and analytics

Platform Capabilities

  • Document verification system
  • Application review and approval tools
  • Fraud detection mechanisms
  • Comprehensive reporting dashboard
  • User management capabilities
