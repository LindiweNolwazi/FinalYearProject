Frequently Asked Questions

Find quick answers to common questions about GoFundMe Connect.

What is GoFundMe Connect?

It's a funding platform created in partnership with the University of Zululand to connect students in need with sponsors who want to support education.

How can students apply for funding?

Students can register, log in to their dashboard, and apply for SRC funds or external sponsorship opportunities directly from the platform.

How can sponsors support students?

Sponsors can register, browse verified student profiles, and fund students based on their criteria such as study program or academic performance.

How are students verified?

All student documents (proof of registration, academic record, fee statements) are verified by University administrators before applications go live.

Is the payment process secure?

Yes. All transactions are processed securely and funds go directly to the University tuition account, not to individuals.

Who can become a sponsor?

Individuals, alumni, companies, NGOs, and foundations are welcome to support students through GoFundMe Connect.
